A Letter To Our Guests

Dear Family and Friends

We are overjoyed to invite you to join us for the wedding of Zalmy and Feigy in the beautiful city of Paris. Celebrating this special simcha with our closest family and friends means the world to us.

We know that traveling internationally takes extra planning. Whether you're joining us for the entire weekend or simply flying in for the wedding day, we are truly grateful you're considering making the journey to celebrate with us.

From the moment you arrive in Paris until the moment you return home, we hope this weekend feels less like attending an event and more like being welcomed into an unforgettable family celebration.

We hope your only responsibility is to enjoy the celebration while we help make the planning as effortless as possible. Our hospitality team is available to help coordinate accommodations, recommend travel options, answer questions, and assist with planning your stay so you can focus entirely on the joy of the simcha.

With all our love,
The Hirsch and Arnauve Families

“We can't imagine beginning this new chapter without the people who have shaped our lives. Thank you for considering making this journey with us — it truly means more than words can express.”

Zalmy & Feigy
